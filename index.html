The Manhattan Jazz Choir becomes the Blue Note Singers!
After 20 years of existence, the Huninguois ensemble is renewed with a new choirmaster, a new pianist, and also new singers who come to reinforce our group, always with the same desire to share this blue note which gives all its musical color to blues and jazz!
